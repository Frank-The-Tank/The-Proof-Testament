As you may know, Formal Methods is one of the first classes you enroll in as a Computer Science student at Pepperdine University. In this course, you will learn and practice the formal methodology in understanding and solving proofs. However, writing out proofs by hand may be tedious, frustrating, and time-consuming (and your professors may not appreciate your handwriting skills; you know who you are).
